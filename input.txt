Available user input
e,f,r,u, 7h(to move 7 of h), 7hs (to specify moving 7 of heart to stack, if available)
----------------
1. Flip the stock deck
    - enter 'f'
2. Move card
    - enter the name of card, i.e. "7s" means 7 of Spade
    - {'d', 'c', 'h', 's'} for Diamond, Club, Heart, Spade
3. Specify moving to stack
    - enter the name of card + 's', i.e. "7ss"
4. 'e' for exit
5. 'u' for undo
6. 'r' for redo
